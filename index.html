About Maroken

Dedicated to restoring independence and improving quality of life through expert prosthetic and orthotic care.

MEET THE OWNER

Maroken Orthotics & Prosthetics is owned by Oratile Maroke, a qualified Medical Orthotist and Prosthetist. She holds a Bachelor of Health Sciences degree in Medical Orthotics and Prosthetics and is dedicated to providing personalized, high-quality prosthetic and orthotic solutions to improve mobility and quality of life.

Learn More About Our Approach

Our mission is simple: every patient deserves professional, compassionate care. We work closely with each individual to understand their specific needs, lifestyle, and goals, ensuring that every device we create is tailored for maximum comfort and functionality.

Our team of certified prosthetists and orthotists stays updated with the latest technologies and techniques in the field. We use only the highest quality materials and employ advanced fitting methods to ensure optimal results for every patient.
